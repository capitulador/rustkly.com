what
happened?

on june 26, 2025 i completely disassociated myself from the last remaining development teams to which i belonged, and with that, i officially retired from the platform. since a long time ago i became a supporter of internet freedom and open source, and with that, i realized that at the end of the day it would not be a bad idea to give users, or developers for that matter, the ease of getting quality assets, their own, without falling into scams, without ads or malicious offers. just browse, find and download.

in the future i would like to give an opportunity to the community, yes, that users can ask me to upload their own assets in order to be shared with the rest of the users and generate an atmosphere of solidarity, which is the intention of this page.

about me? this is not the end, in fact, it is the beginning of something better, it is the beginning of leading processes of free and free acquisition and commercialization for all, it is the moment to impact more deeply in the community, it is my way to show my appreciation and gratitude to the community <3

thank you for being there, thank you for allowing me to demonstrate my abilities, thank you for not evaluating me for my limitations, thank you for everything.
